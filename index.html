About

Wrench is one of the main character from the game Watch Dogs 2 and I decided it would be a fun project to create a real version of his light up mask.

Although there are already some great tutorials out there, such as this one by R-I-H-A-M or another by Johnno376I, I felt there was room for improvement and so built and coded my own using them as inspiration. The build took a year, but was totally worth it and so felt I should document the results in a tutorial of my own!

This version:

  • Is controlled wirelessly over bluetooth by a custom built Android application
  • Has 48 different set expressions with blinking and transition animations
  • Also allows you to draw custom expressions, display messages and adjust speed and brightness
  • Looks more like the in-game character than most other tutorials

Steps

Step 1 - Preparing the mask

To start, I bought a cheap Wrench mask from eBay. The shape is a good immitation of the in-game mask, but the paint job was awful. I therefore spray painted the whole thing black, painted the spikes and bit at the top silver and then coated it in a clear gloss to make it more durable.
