About

Inspired by a tutorial on Instructables, I decided to make my own bluetooth controlled Wrench mask. This site documents the steps I took.


Parts

What you'll need:

Plastic Wrench mask
Black spray paint
Metallic silver paint + paintbrush
Metallic silver paint
Arduino Uno
USB A to USB B cable
x2 white 8x8 LED matrix with module
9V battery holder with connector lead
x14 male to female jumper wire, ideally 5 should be black
Velcro strip
Black electric tape
Liquid nails
